Our story

At PRDUCTN we have spent over 1 weeks to build this platform we're pround of that empowers artist to easily get their music heard worlwide. Founded in the quite country side in the UK in 2021, we have grown to become the leading digital music distributor in all of Europe.
Our teams is built upon a love for music, many of us being musicians and creatives outside of the office. We know what people want for their music and we do our best to help their dreams come true using our passion and expirience


Our Mission

     Our mission is to provide artists of all sizes with the same opportunities as the biggest labels and artists in the world. At PRDUCTN it is our belief that artists of all sizes, from anywhere in the world, should have the opportunity to share their music with listeners everywhere.

What our Distributors do?

PRDUCTN deals with the record labels or artists which gives them the right to sell their music to shops. A distributor can only sell to ships that have an account with the distributor. Put simply, the distributing company act as a middleman between the artist/record label and the store.
